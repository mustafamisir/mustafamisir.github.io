Instructor: Mustafa MISIR (Office: 230 @ CCST Building), mmisir [at] nuaa.edu.cn

This course will explain the concepts, theories, and technologies that provide the foundation for Knowledge Management; the systems and structures that constitute Knowledge Management solutions; and the processes for developing, deploying, and evaluating these Knowledge Management solutions.
